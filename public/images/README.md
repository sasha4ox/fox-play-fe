# Logo images

Place your FoxyPlay logo images here with these exact filenames:

- **logo-orange-horizontal.png** – Orange & white logo, horizontal layout (fox + text side by side) – used in **light** theme
- **logo-purple-horizontal.png** – Purple & lime logo, horizontal layout – used in **dark** theme

Optional (for future use):
- **logo-orange-stacked.png** – Orange & white, stacked layout
- **logo-purple-stacked.png** – Purple & lime, stacked layout

All logos are designed for dark backgrounds and display best in dark theme.
